THE BLACK STONE GROUP



Private investment banking company

  • Blackstone is an American alternative investment management company based in New York City.
  • Blackstone is a private equity firm that inests in leveraged buyouts, real estate, credit, infrastructure, hedge funds, insurance, secondaries and growth equity
  • Blackstone's mission is to deliver long-term capital preservation and enhanced returns
  • Blackstone has invested in companies such as Hilton Worldwide, Merlin Entertainments Group, Performance Food Group, and Equity Office Properties
  • Blackstone was founded in 1985 by Stephen A. Schwarzman and Peter Peterson
  • Blackstone has about $500 billion in assests under management
